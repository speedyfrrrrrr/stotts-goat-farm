Who We Are

The Stotts Family and Their Farm

We are a small family farm located just outside of Columbia, Kentucky. This whole thing started because we love animals and we love being outside. One thing led to another and here we are making goat milk soap and having the time of our lives doing it.

Every morning we are out there feeding the goats, checking on them, and taking care of the land. It is a lot of work but we genuinely enjoy every bit of it. These animals are part of our family.

The soap came naturally from all of that. We had the milk, we had the time, and we wanted to put it to good use. Every bar we make has a little piece of this farm in it and we hope you can feel that.
